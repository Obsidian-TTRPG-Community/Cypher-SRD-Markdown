# Enveloping Shield

**Type**: Enabler

Your [[Compendium/Cypher/Abilities/F/Force-Field-Shield|Force Field Shield]] ability produces an envelope of force that enfolds you while you are holding the shield, granting you +1 to Armor. Enabler.
